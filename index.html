Hi, I’m Fabio.

Data scientist · Urban energy modeler · Builder of thoughtful tools for cities.

I translate a PhD in Urban Building Energy Modeling into production-ready analytics. Most days I design data workflows that help public agencies, utilities, and product teams invest in retrofit programs, responsible AI, and resilient energy systems.

Research roots

My doctoral work explored how large-scale digital twins and physics-driven calibration can support decarbonization at the district scale. That research now informs the products I ship and the stories I tell with data.

Industry focus

Today I partner with cross-functional teams to make messy urban data understandable. Recent work spans demand forecasting, retrofit targeting, and geospatial analytics for mobility and energy transition teams.
